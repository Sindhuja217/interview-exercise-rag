# Domain Suspension Triage Runbook

This runbook provides step-by-step guidance for support agents when handling domain suspension inquiries.

---

## 1. Identify Suspension Reason

- Review account notes, system flags, and recent notifications
- Determine whether the suspension is related to WHOIS, abuse, billing, or legal action
- Confirm the current domain status and registry state

---

## 2. WHOIS-Related Suspensions

If the suspension is due to WHOIS issues:

- Ask the customer to update inaccurate or missing WHOIS information
- Confirm that WHOIS email verification has been completed
- Inform the customer that reactivation typically occurs within **24 hours** after verification

---

## 3. Abuse-Related Suspensions

If the suspension is related to abuse or policy violations:

- Do **not** provide a reactivation ETA
- Do **not** manually reactivate the domain
- Escalate the case to the **Abuse Team** with all relevant details

---

## 4. Billing-Related Suspensions

If the suspension is related to billing issues:

- Confirm payment status and outstanding balances
- Guide the customer through payment resolution if applicable
- Escalate to the **Billing Team** if payment issues persist
