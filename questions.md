# Support Action Classification – Test Questions

This document contains labeled test questions used to evaluate action routing for the Support Knowledge Assistant.

---

1. What is WHOIS, and what type of information does it typically contain?
2. Why are domains locked by default after registration?
3. My domain was suspended because of unverified WHOIS information. What do I need to do to restore it?
4. My domain is locked and I want to transfer it. What steps should I take?
5. I’ve completed WHOIS email verification. How long should I wait before my domain is reactivated?
6. I renewed my domain during the grace period. When should my services come back online?
7. My domain was suspended due to missing WHOIS details, but I believe my information is correct. Can you check?
8. I updated my WHOIS info, but my domain is still suspended after 24 hours.
9. My domain was suspended for phishing, but I’ve already removed the content. Can you reactivate it now?
10. I received an abuse complaint against my domain and need to know what remediation evidence is required.
11. I was charged twice for a domain renewal. Can I get a refund?
12. My domain was suspended due to payment failure, but I believe my payment went through.
13. My domain shows as active, but DNS is not resolving even after renewal.
14. I renewed my domain successfully, but services are still offline outside the expected restoration window.
15. My domain is suspended, but I don’t know why. Can you help?
16. I want to dispute a billing charge, but I don’t have the invoice number yet.
